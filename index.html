🐾 About Pet-Friendly City

Pet-Friendly City is a volunteer-driven campaign that dreams of cities where street animals are protected, loved, and respected. Whether it's rescuing a pup, feeding a stray, or spreading awareness — we're here to make tails wag and hearts melt.

Our mission is to turn every neighborhood into a safe and happy space for our furry friends. With your help, we can build a kinder, more compassionate world — one paw at a time. 🐕🌍
